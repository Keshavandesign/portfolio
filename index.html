Problem Studies.

Design Philosophy

"I used to fight for pixel-perfect at every breakpoint. Then I started shipping."

The real skill isn't perfection. It's knowing exactly what belongs in v1 and what's ego. Ship-fast culture is fine until it stops giving the team time to think. The job is holding the line on the right things. Not all things.
